Investigation Overview¶

The overall goal of this presentation is to show a glimpse of how some of the other factors influenced our major variable of interest (duration_sec).

Key insights to be observed in this presentation are:

How user type influenced average duration.

How member gender influenced average duration.

How bike_share_for_all_trips influenced average duration.

How member_gender(Male and Female), user_type and how they relate with duration_sec on an average.

Dataset Overview¶

This document explores a dataset containing information about individual rides made in a bike sharing system covering the greater San Francisco Bay area.
